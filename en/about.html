Our Mission

We aim to provide a secure platform for whistleblowers while ensuring transparency and accountability in all areas of public and private interest. Our commitment is to protect those who come forward with critical information and to make that information accessible for public scrutiny and action.

Sector-7 was founded on the principle that informed citizens are essential for a just society. We leverage cutting-edge security practices and advocate for the use of anonymity tools like Tor to safeguard our users.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct.
  • Security: Prioritizing the safety and anonymity of our whistleblowers.
  • Transparency: Promoting openness and access to information.
  • Accountability: Working to ensure that institutions and individuals are answerable for their actions.
  • Courage: Supporting the bravery of those who speak out against wrongdoing.
