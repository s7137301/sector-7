Our Mission

We aim to provide a secure platform for whistleblowers while ensuring transparency and accountability.

What We Do

Sector-7 facilitates the secure collection and distribution of sensitive information. Through robust encryption, cutting-edge cybersecurity practices, and collaboration with trustworthy networks, we ensure the confidentiality of whistleblowers while disseminating vital information to the public.

Our Commitment to Security

At Sector-7, the safety of whistleblowers and their data is paramount. We employ state-of-the-art security measures, including dedicated onion services and collaboration with leading cybersecurity organizations, to withstand threats and ensure anonymity. In case of disruptions, we work diligently to restore services while preserving the integrity of all data.

Why We Were Founded

Sector-7 was established to provide a secure and anonymous platform for individuals who possess information about unethical, illegal, or harmful activities. Our mission is to empower whistleblowers and truth-seekers with the ability to report and share critical evidence without fear of retribution or compromise.

Recognizing the immense challenges faced by those who choose to expose wrongdoing, we strive to protect their anonymity and safeguard their information through advanced security measures. Sector-7 exists as a beacon for transparency and accountability, ensuring that voices demanding justice are heard and supported globally.
